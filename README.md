Netflix Data Analysis Project:
# Project Overview

    This project analyzes Netflix’s content catalog using Python to uncover insights about content distribution, trends, and growth over time. The analysis focuses on understanding the types of content available and global production patterns.

# Objectives
    Analyze the distribution of Movies vs TV Shows
    Identify top content-producing countries
    Discover the most popular genres
    Examine trends in content addition over time
    Explore release year patterns

# Tools & Technologies
    Python
    Pandas
    Matplotlib

# Dataset

The dataset contains information about Netflix titles, including:

    Show ID
    Type (Movie/TV Show)
    Title
    Director
    Country
    Date Added
    Release Year
    Rating
    Duration

# Key Visualizations & Insights
1. Movies vs TV Shows
Movies dominate the Netflix catalog
TV Shows are growing steadily, indicating increased demand for series content
2. Top Content-Producing Countries
The United States leads in content production
Other major contributors include India and the United Kingdom
Reflects Netflix’s global expansion strategy
3. Content Added Over Time
Significant growth after 2015
Indicates rapid expansion of Netflix’s library
4. Release Year Trends
Majority of content is from recent years
Mix of modern and older titles available

# Conclusion

The analysis highlights Netflix’s strong growth in content over the years, with a clear focus on global expansion . The platform continues to balance movies and TV shows while increasing its investment in international and recent content.
